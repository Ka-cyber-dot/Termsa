Katale User Data & Account Management

At Katale, we value your privacy and give you full control over your personal information and merchant profile data.

How to Edit or Delete Your Data

You can completely modify or remove your information directly inside the application at any time:

  • To Edit: Navigate to your Profile Settings within the app to update your account details instantly.
  • To Delete: Go to your Profile Settings and select the option to delete your account. This will permanently purge your credentials and associated data from our systems.
